The Founder

The Story Behind All ONDECK HUB

I grew up playing tennis in Nigeria with borrowed rackets and borrowed court time. I had the talent — but I didn't have access to the coaches, resources, or networks that players in Europe and America took for granted.

After years of grinding, I earned my ITF Level 1 and US PTR coaching certifications, completed diplomas in Sport Coaching & Leadership and Business Management & Entrepreneurship, and coached at academies in Nigeria and abroad, helping dozens of players reach their potential. But I kept seeing the same problem: talented African players falling through the cracks simply because they couldn't afford quality coaching or didn't have the visibility to attract scouts.

In 2021, I launched All ONDECK TENNIS Hub — and in 2024, it evolved into All ONDECK HUB: a fully digital platform designed to bring world-class tennis development to every African player, wherever they are.

"If I'd had this platform when I was 14, my career would have looked very different. I'm building it so the next generation doesn't have to wish for what we didn't have."

👨
Coach Kazeem Rasaki
Founder & Head Coach · All ONDECK HUB

The Journey

From Court to Digital Platform

A decade of coaching on African courts led to a simple realisation: the biggest barrier to African tennis talent isn't ability — it's access. Here's how All ONDECK HUB was built.

2013

Started Coaching Career

Earned ITF Level 1 certification and US PTR certification. Began coaching juniors while pursuing diplomas in Sport Coaching & Leadership and Business Management.

2016

PTR Certified & NTF Partner

Achieved US PTR certification and joined the Nigeria Tennis Federation as a junior development partner coach.

2019

First Scholarship Placement

Guided first student to a US college tennis scholarship (NCAA D2). Saw the gap in structured scholarship support for African players.

2021

Launched All ONDECK TENNIS Hub

Started as a WhatsApp community and YouTube channel. Quickly grew to 500+ members across Nigeria, Ghana, and the UK diaspora.

2024

All ONDECK HUB Launched

Evolved into a full digital platform: online courses, talent showcase, virtual coaching, and paid mentorship community.

2026

2,400+ Students. 18 Countries.

Now serving players, parents, and coaches across 18 African countries and the global African diaspora.
