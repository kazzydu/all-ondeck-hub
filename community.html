What's Inside

Everything You Need to Develop Faster

The Edge Tennis community is more than a chat group — it's a structured development ecosystem designed to accelerate your progress.

🎙️

Weekly Live Q&A Sessions

Every Thursday, Pro and Elite members join a live video call with our head coaches. Ask anything — technique, tactics, tournaments, scholarships, injuries.

🏆

Weekly Training Challenges

New challenges drop every Monday. Film your attempt, post in the community, and get feedback from coaches and peers. Top performers get featured on our showcase.

🎓

Scholarship & Tournament Alerts

Be first to know about US college recruitment deadlines, ITF junior tournament registrations, and African continental championship opportunities.

📞

Monthly 1-on-1 Mentorship Call

Pro members get a 30-minute personal call with a coach each month. Set goals, review progress, and get personalised guidance for your specific development needs.

📚

Unlimited Course Access

Pro and Elite members unlock all 24 courses in the library with no individual purchase needed. New courses added monthly at no extra cost.

👤

Talent Showcase Profile

Pro members get a verified showcase profile with enhanced visibility, a Pro badge, and the ability to receive direct messages from scouts and coaches.

💬

Private Community Forum

Connect with players, parents, and coaches from across Africa and the diaspora. Share wins, ask questions, find hitting partners, and stay motivated.

📊

Progress Tracking Dashboard

Log your UTR, tournament results, fitness scores, and training sessions. See your development over time and share milestones with the community.
